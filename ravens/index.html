Fanatics

Who Are We?

Fanatics is a leading online retailer of officially licensed sports merchandise and provides the ultimate shopping experience to sports fans. As a Top 50 Internet Retailer Company, Fanatics comprises the broadest online assortment offering hundreds of thousands of officially licensed items via its Fanatics (www.fanatics.com) and FansEdge (www.fansedge.com) brands. In addition, the Company powers the e-commerce sites of all major professional sports leagues (NFL, MLB, NBA, NHL, NASCAR, PGA), major media brands (NBC Sports, CBS Sports, FOX Sports) and over 200 collegiate and professional team properties. With thousands of employees throughout multiple states and more than 1 million square feet of warehousing space, every member of the Fanatics family is dedicated to the customer’s 100% satisfaction.

Why Shop With Us?

Value. Quality. Service. Commitment. Fanatics realizes that in order to be successful in the sports merchandising industry, we must provide our customers with great prices, superior products and impeccable service.


What Do We Sell?

We offer one of the world’s leading assortments of officially licensed sports merchandise for over 700 teams across multiple leagues: NCAA, NFL, MLB, NBA, NASCAR, NHL, Soccer, Olympics, MMA, Action Sports and more. Our unique team and league-licensed products range from men’s, women’s, and kids’ apparel, gameday gear, accessories and more that totals over 250,000 items. We proudly provide the most trusted name brands including Nike, Reebok, adidas, Cutter & Buck, Champion, Antigua, New Era, Russell Athletic, Tommy Hilfiger, Twins, Zephyr, Colosseum, Lee, Under Armour and more.
